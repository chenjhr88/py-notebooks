Seattle


Seattle is a city located in Washington. Seattle is located near the Puget Sound of Washington.This state is located at the Northwest corner of the USA. Washington contains many mountainous regions, beaches, and lakes. Washington is home to many businesses such as Boeing, Microsoft, Amazon, and Eddie Bauer. Seattle was first created as a reservation for the Indians. The tribe that had lived there was displaced by the new settlers. After living in poverty, the Indian tribe was almost wiped out.
